# Privacy Policy for EXIF Renamer

**Last updated:** March 2026

## Overview

EXIF Renamer is a photo renaming app that uses metadata (EXIF) from your files to generate new filenames. This policy describes what data the app uses and where it goes.

## Data the App Uses

- **Files and folders:** The app only accesses files or folders that you explicitly select. It does not access your full Photo Library or other device storage without your choice.

- **EXIF metadata:** The app reads metadata (such as date, time, camera model, and location) from the files you select. This is used only to build new filenames and show you a preview. This data stays on your device.

- **Location in filenames:** If you use the optional location feature, GPS coordinates stored in your photo metadata may be sent to **Apple's** geocoding service to obtain place names (e.g. city, country). This is done via Apple's built-in services on your device. We do not receive, store, or have access to this data.

## What We Do Not Do

- We do not collect, store, or sell your personal data.
- The app does not use accounts or sign-in.
- We do not use third-party advertising or analytics that track you.

## Contact
